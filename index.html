OUR LEADERS

 
 
 
 
Rachel Ballatori, Founder + CEO

Thinker. Creator. Originator. Dog mom. Foodie. Ask Rachel if she’s driven by her left brain, her right brain, or her heart and she’ll answer, “Yes.”

Which is why after spending her early career overseeing accounts, devising killer strategy, creating award-winning content, and crushing social engagement metrics for several travel, tourism, food and beverage brands—all while starting her own non-profit donating several cancer research grants to Johns Hopkins Hospital—it’s no surprise she was named one of Baltimore’s Finest 35 Under 35.

Along the way she witnessed what makes brands soar and where they have to settle in both advertising and media publishing, then she set out on her own to build a better way. Mill + Co.

 
 
 
 
Emily Apple, Account Manager

Emily is fiercely organized and loves a good color-coded spreadsheet. As a professional dancer and photographer, storytelling has always been her passion.

In 2021, she earned a Master’s of Science in Digital Communications degree with a specialization in Advertising from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University. Emily holds certifications in Social Media Marketing from HubSpot Academy, Marketing Foundations from The Trade Desk Edge Academy, and Display Advertising from Google Ads. She is excited to help bring your brand’s story to life and will be there for you every step of the way.

 
 
 
 
Olivia Sun, Assistant Account Manager

Driven by passion and curiosity, Olivia is a firm believer in the idea of “Living life to its fullest”.

Born and raised in Shanghai, China, she graduated from Boston University’s College of Communications with a Master’s degree in Advertising in 2022. Before joining Mill + Co., she has experience working in Account Management on major CPG brands during her internships and she holds certifications such as Advanced Google Analytics from Google Analytics Academy. Olivia is determined to help better tell your brand’s story and satisfy your brand’s needs.

Outside of her professional life, you can catch Olivia enjoying music, Hip-Hop dancing, and a scented candle before bed.
